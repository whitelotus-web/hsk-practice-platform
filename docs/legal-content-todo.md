# Legal / Content TODO For Production Clone

The product model can be cloned and improved. The following parts must not be
copied from SuperTest/HSKOnline or any other protected source without permission.

## Do Not Copy

- Logo, icon, app name, domain, company name, visual marks.
- Brand colors and trade dress if the result would confuse users.
- Screenshots, illustrations, QR codes, app store badges from their site.
- Paid question bank, mock tests, past exam packs, answers, explanations.
- Audio files, transcripts, images, teacher notes, essay samples.
- User reviews/testimonials unless independently licensed.
- Pricing page wording, legal policy wording, refund wording.
- User/account data, comments, leaderboard, messages.

## Fill In With Our Own Assets

- Final app name and logo.
- Company legal name, address, support email.
- Original UI theme and design system after initial functional parity.
- Original or licensed HSK question bank.
- Original or licensed HSK 7-9 Advanced mock packs with 98 questions per full
  set.
- Native-speaker audio recordings.
- Native-speaker audio recordings and teacher-reviewed speaking prompts for HSK
  7-9.
- Original picture assets for picture matching and writing prompts.
- Officially licensed exam-style mock packs where required.
- Vietnamese-first explanations, grammar notes and learning tips.
- Teacher-reviewed HSK 7-9 translation/speaking/writing rubrics.
- Teacher correction process, pricing and terms.

## Current Repo Handling

- `static-app/data.js` uses original starter content written for this project.
- HSK 7-9 content in `static-app/data.js` is seed content for product flow
  validation, not a full official paper.
- Content Admin can export a 98-question Advanced template, but every TODO item
  must be replaced with original/licensed content and teacher-reviewed rubric
  before publication.
- Items marked `assetStatus` need production audio/image assets.
- `protectedCloneTodo` lists content and business inputs still required.
- UI follows the same product structure but is not a pixel clone.
